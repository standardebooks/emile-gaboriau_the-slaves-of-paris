The Slaves of Paris
was published in 1868 by
Emile Gaboriau.
It was translated from French in 1913 by
Charles Scribner’s Sons.

This ebook was produced for the
Standard Ebooks project
by
Vince Rice,
and is based on a transcription produced in 2006 by
Dagny, John Bickers, David Widger, and Emma Dudding
for
Project Gutenberg
and on digital scans available at
Google Books.

The cover page is adapted from
The Boulevard Montmartre on a Winter Morning,
a painting completed in 1897 by
Camille Pissarro.
The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.
